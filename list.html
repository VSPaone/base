LIST

A list is a data structure that allows you to organize and store multiple items in a specific order. Lists are commonly used in programming and computer science to manage collections of related elements, such as numbers, strings, or objects. They provide a convenient way to access, iterate over, and manipulate data. In programming languages, lists often offer flexibility and versatility, allowing for dynamic resizing, insertion, and deletion of elements. Lists are fundamental in various algorithms and are essential for tasks ranging from simple data storage to complex data processing. Overall, lists are a foundational concept in computer science, aiding in the efficient management and manipulation of data.
